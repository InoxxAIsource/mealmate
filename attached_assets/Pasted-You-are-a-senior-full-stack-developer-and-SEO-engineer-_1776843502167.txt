FIX 1 — DUPLICATE BRAND NAME IN PAGE TITLES (CRITICAL)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PROBLEM: Sub-page titles render as:
  "PCOS Meal Plan India — Hormone-Friendly Indian Meals | MealCoreAI | MealCoreAI"
  "Health & Nutrition Blog for Indian Families | MealCoreAI | MealCoreAI"
The brand name appears twice. This is caused by the root layout metadata template 
conflicting with page-level titles that already include the brand name.

FIX:
In your root layout.tsx, set the metadata title template like this:

  export const metadata: Metadata = {
    title: {
      default: 'MealCoreAI — AI Meal Planning for PCOS, Diabetes, Thyroid, Pregnancy & Kids',
      template: '%s | MealCoreAI',
    },
    ...
  }

Then in every page.tsx that currently includes "| MealCoreAI" or "MealCoreAI" at the end 
of its title string, REMOVE the brand suffix. The template will append it automatically.

Example — change this:
  title: 'PCOS Meal Plan India — Hormone-Friendly Indian Meals | MealCoreAI'
To this:
  title: 'PCOS Meal Plan India — Hormone-Friendly Indian Meals'

Apply this fix to ALL pages: /pcos-meal-plan, /diabetes-meal-plan, /thyroid-diet-plan, 
/pregnancy-meal-plan, /kids-meal-plan, /cholesterol-diet-plan, /blog, /about, /faq, 
/pricing, /how-it-works, and all /blog/[slug] pages.

Also audit title lengths — ideal is under 60 characters. If any title exceeds 60 chars 
after removing the brand suffix, shorten it to under 60 characters while keeping the 
primary keyword at the start.
